The Best Movies According to Zanele

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

1. Creed

The amount of action and competitiveness between the characters is extremely
exciting to see. The family dynamics as well as the hardships the main character
faced make it the production relatable for some because it is a classic story of
being an underdog, having a well-known father and not wanting to be labelled as
a 'nepo-baby'.

2. The Old Guard

The movie a great time-travelling experience and showcases supernatural power
of immortality with unexpected twist which not only captivates you with the amount
of action and tugs at your heart strings with the some of the emotional scenes.

3. The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas

The tragic and tear-jerking movie set during World War II. The story is about an young
boy named Bruno who befriends another young boy, Shmuel, on the other side of
the fence a concentration camp, unaware of the true and unjust nature of the camp.
